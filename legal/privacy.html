1. Who we are

"Huddlex" (also referred to as "we", "us", or "our") is a mobile application that lets you share photos and short video clips inside small private communities. Photos and videos shared on Huddlex automatically disappear six (6) hours after they are posted.

This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect when you use Huddlex, why we collect it, how we use it, and the rights you have under applicable law — including the Saudi Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL), the European Union General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

2. What information we collect

2.1 Information you provide directly

  • Account information: your email address, a password (encrypted in transit and at rest), your chosen display name, and an optional profile photo.
  • Communities and memberships: the communities (private groups) you create or join, your role inside them (member, admin, trusted user), and your active/favorite preferences.
  • Posts (huddles): the photos, short video clips (max 6 seconds, audio stripped before upload), and optional captions you share with your communities.
  • Likes and interactions: which posts you have liked and which users you have blocked or reported.
  • Reports: when you report content or a user, we store the reason you provided so a moderator can review it.
  • Verification codes: when you sign up or sign in with email, we generate a 6-digit code and store a one-way hash of it briefly (up to 10 minutes) so we can confirm it's really you.

2.2 Information collected automatically

  • Device and technical information: device model, operating system version, app version, time zone, and a Firebase Installations ID used to anonymously identify your app instance for service purposes.
  • Crash and diagnostic data: if the app crashes, our backend (Firebase Crashlytics, if enabled in a future version) may receive technical information about the crash. No content from your posts is included in crash reports.
  • Connection state: the app uses the device's connectivity API to detect when you go offline, so it can show an offline banner. This data stays on the device.

2.3 Information from third parties

If you choose to sign in with Google, Google will share your email address and basic profile information with us as needed to create your Huddlex account. We never receive your Google password.

3. How we use your information

We use your information solely to operate and improve Huddlex. Specifically:

  • To create and maintain your account, sign you in, and recover access (e.g., password reset).
  • To deliver the core service — letting you share posts inside communities and receive posts from people in your communities.
  • To send transactional emails such as the 6-digit verification code used during sign-up.
  • To enforce our Terms of Service, investigate reports of abuse, and protect Huddlex and its users from harmful or unlawful content.
  • To debug and improve the app's reliability.
  • To comply with our legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data for behavioral advertising, and we do not share it with advertising networks.

4. Legal bases for processing

Where the GDPR or the Saudi PDPL applies, we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract: to provide the Huddlex service you signed up for.
  • Consent: for sign-up, optional features, and any future use that is not strictly necessary to provide the service.
  • Legitimate interests: to protect the platform from abuse, debug errors, and operate the service safely. We balance these interests against your privacy rights.
  • Legal obligation: to respond to lawful requests from authorities.

6. International data transfers

Huddlex's backend services are operated by Google through Firebase. Google may process and store data in data centers located outside the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, including in the United States and the European Union. By using Huddlex, you understand that your information may be transferred to and stored in countries with data-protection laws that differ from those in your country of residence.

Where required by the Saudi PDPL or the GDPR, transfers are performed under appropriate safeguards, including Google's contractual commitments to comply with international data protection standards.

7. Data retention

We keep your information only as long as we need it:

  • Posts (huddles) — photos and video clips: automatically deleted approximately six (6) hours after they are posted. This deletion is enforced by our backend; you do not need to do anything.
  • Account information (email, name, profile photo, community memberships): kept while your account is active. Deleted when you delete your account from inside the app.
  • Verification codes: deleted as soon as the code is used, expires (10 minutes), or fails too many times. Only a one-way hash of the code is stored — never the code itself.
  • Reports: kept indefinitely so moderators can review repeat offenders. Reports are never visible to other end users.
  • Diagnostic and crash data: typically retained for up to 90 days by our service providers.

8. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you have the following rights:

  • Access: request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: ask us to correct inaccurate information. You can also edit your display name and profile photo at any time from inside the app.
  • Deletion: delete your account and all your data. You can do this yourself from Account → Delete my account inside the app. All your account data, memberships, likes, and favorites are removed immediately; posts you shared expire naturally within 6 hours.
  • Portability: receive your data in a machine-readable format. Email us at the address below.
  • Objection and restriction: object to certain processing or ask us to restrict it.
  • Withdraw consent: where we rely on your consent, you can withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of past processing.
  • Lodge a complaint: with your local data-protection regulator. In Saudi Arabia, that is the Saudi Data and Artificial Intelligence Authority (SDAIA). In the EU, your national data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@huddlex.app. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your information, including TLS encryption for data in transit, Firebase Authentication for credential storage, server-side Firestore security rules to enforce access control, and one-way hashing of email verification codes. We restrict internal access to user data to what is strictly necessary to operate the service.

No system is completely secure. If we become aware of a security incident affecting your data, we will notify you and any required regulator in accordance with applicable law.

10. Children's privacy

Huddlex is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@huddlex.app and we will delete the account and associated data.

In some jurisdictions, including the European Union and the United Kingdom, the minimum age for consent to data processing is higher (typically 16). Users in those jurisdictions must meet the local minimum age, or have a parent or legal guardian provide consent on their behalf.

11. Cookies and similar technologies

Huddlex is a mobile application and does not use web cookies. We do, however, use local device storage (Shared Preferences on iOS/Android) to remember your language preference, sign-in session, and whether you have completed the onboarding tutorial. You can clear this data at any time by signing out or uninstalling the app.

We do not use Apple's Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA) or Google's Advertising ID. We do not track you across apps or websites.
